I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkustnlp.github.io/), supervised by [Professor Junxian He](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~junxianh/). I previously worked with Professor Junxian He during my undergraduate studies at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research focuses on **natural language processing** and **machine learning**. My specific research interests include:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**
