## Research Interests

My research interests span several areas within natural language processing and machine learning:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Developing methods to improve logical reasoning capabilities in large language models
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models**: Investigating and mitigating hallucination problems in multimodal AI systems
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Understanding and improving the truthfulness and interpretability of large language models
